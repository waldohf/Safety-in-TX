Howdy! Safety in Texas is a website built to display safety related Twitter data from the major cities in Texas. Our backend periodically crawls Twitter and uses Naive Bayes to classify the data. Our frontend is built off of the Django 1.6 web framework. We have collected approximately 120,000 Tweets and counting. Feel free to have a look around!
